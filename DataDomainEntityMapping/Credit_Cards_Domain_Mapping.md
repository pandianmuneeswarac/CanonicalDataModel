# Credit Cards - Domain to Entity Mapping

## Data Domain to Entity Mapping

| Serial Number | Data Domain Name | Entity Names |
|---------------|------------------|-------------|
| 1 | Customer | Credit Card Customer, Cardholder, Primary Cardholder, Authorized User, Customer Credit Profile, Customer Spending Behavior |
| 2 | Account | Credit Card Account, Card Account Balance, Account Statement, Account Status, Account Cycle, Billing Account |
| 3 | Party | Primary Cardholder, Additional Cardholder, Authorized User, Supplementary Cardholder, Emergency Contact |
| 4 | Product | Credit Card Product, Rewards Card, Cash Back Card, Premium Card, Secured Card, Business Card, Student Card, Product Benefits |
| 5 | Transaction | Purchase Transaction, Cash Advance, Balance Transfer, Payment Transaction, Refund Transaction, Chargeback, Dispute Transaction, Authorization |
| 6 | Card | Credit Card, Physical Card, Virtual Card, Digital Wallet Card, Card Embossing, Card PIN, Card CVV, Card Expiry |
| 7 | Loan | Credit Line, Cash Advance Loan, Installment Plan, Balance Transfer Loan, Credit Limit |
| 8 | Collateral | Security Deposit (Secured Cards), Collateral Account |
| 9 | Payment | Card Payment, Minimum Payment, Full Payment, Autopay, Payment Due Date, Payment Method, Payment Allocation |
| 10 | Risk | Credit Risk Score, Fraud Score, Transaction Risk, Account Risk Level, Credit Bureau Data, Delinquency Status, Default Risk |
| 11 | Compliance | Credit Card Agreement, Truth in Lending Disclosure, Privacy Notice, Cardholder Agreement, Regulatory Report, Card Act Compliance |
| 12 | Investment | N/A - Limited credit card investment entities |
| 13 | Channel | Card Activation Channel, Online Card Management, Mobile App, Customer Service, Card Replacement Channel |
| 14 | Limit | Credit Limit, Cash Advance Limit, Daily Transaction Limit, International Transaction Limit, ATM Withdrawal Limit, Over Limit |
| 15 | Fee | Annual Fee, Late Payment Fee, Over Limit Fee, Foreign Transaction Fee, Cash Advance Fee, Balance Transfer Fee, Returned Payment Fee |
| 16 | Interest | Purchase APR, Cash Advance APR, Balance Transfer APR, Penalty APR, Interest Charge, Grace Period, Interest Rate Tier |
| 17 | Document | Card Application, Credit Agreement, Monthly Statement, Payment Confirmation, Dispute Letter, Card Terms and Conditions |
| 18 | Organization | Card Operations, Credit Card Division, Collections Department, Fraud Department, Customer Service Center |
| 19 | Employee | Card Specialist, Collections Agent, Fraud Analyst, Underwriter, Customer Service Agent |
| 20 | Location | Card Mailing Address, Billing Address, Statement Address, Merchant Location, Transaction Location |
| 21 | Treasury | N/A - Limited credit card treasury entities |
| 22 | Pricing | APR Pricing, Fee Schedule, Promotional Rate, Introductory Rate, Penalty Pricing, Rewards Pricing |
| 23 | Event | Card Activation, Card Cancellation, Credit Limit Increase, Credit Limit Decrease, Payment Event, Dispute Event, Fraud Event |
| 24 | Reference | Card Network Code, Merchant Category Code, Transaction Type Code, Decline Reason Code, Currency Code, Country Code |
| 25 | Analytics | Customer Spending Pattern, Rewards Redemption Rate, Attrition Risk, Credit Utilization, Profitability Score, Lifetime Value |
